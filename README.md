Voice of Jhang – Digital Civic Intelligence Platform
Overview
Voice of Jhang is a regional civic information and community support platform scaled organically to 105,000+ followers.
This repository documents the analytical framework, digital growth strategy, and structured engagement monitoring model behind the platform’s development.
Rather than functioning solely as a media outlet, Voice of Jhang operates as a civic intelligence system focused on identifying community concerns, monitoring engagement behavior, and improving public information flow using structured observation techniques.

📈 Analytical Framework

The platform operates using a structured observation model:
1. Topic Categorization
Posts are grouped into civic categories such as:
Infrastructure issues
Public safety concerns
Utility/service disruptions
Community announcements

2. Engagement Velocity Tracking
Monitoring:
Early engagement rate
Peak interaction window
Comment-to-reaction ratio

3. Sentiment Clustering (Manual Observational Model)
Comments are reviewed and categorized into:
Supportive
Concerned
Critical
Informational

4. Behavioral Pattern Mapping
Recurring patterns are identified based on:
Posting time
Issue type
Engagement density
Share velocity
